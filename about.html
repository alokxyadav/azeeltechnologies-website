Our Story

From a Bold Vision to India's Leading Digital Agency

Azeel Technologies was born from a simple but powerful belief: every business — regardless of size or location — deserves access to world-class digital solutions. Founded by Alok Yadav (Alok Kumar Yadav) in Bihar, India, our journey began with one client and one promise: to deliver results that matter.

Today, we operate across two hubs — our headquarters in Gaya, Bihar and our development centre at IIIT Manipur — and serve businesses across India and internationally with a team of passionate engineers, marketers, and AI specialists.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every strategy we craft, every line of code we write, and every campaign we run is purpose-built for your business goals — no templates, no shortcuts.
