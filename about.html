Who We Are

Your Premier Full-Service Digital Partner

Azeel Technologies is a premier, full-service digital agency specialising in crafting custom digital solutions that blend stunning design, robust engineering, and strategic growth marketing.

Founded and headquartered in Bihar, India, with a dedicated development centre at IIIT Manipur, we bring together a passionate team of engineers, designers, marketers, and AI specialists united by a single purpose: to help businesses grow, scale, and lead in the digital world.

Our clients range from ambitious startups to established enterprises across industries including healthcare, finance, education, e-commerce, logistics, and media — all united by their desire to leverage technology as a true growth engine.

How We Got Here

From a spark of an idea to a trusted digital agency — every milestone has shaped who we are today.

Azeel Technologies Founded

Alok Ranjan establishes Azeel Technologies with a clear mission: to deliver world-class digital services to businesses across India and help them compete globally.

2023 — Early Days

First Client Projects Delivered

Azeel delivers its first wave of web development and digital marketing projects, earning 5-star reviews and establishing a reputation for reliability and quality.

2024

AI & Automation Services Launched

Responding to growing demand, Azeel expands into AI automation — helping clients streamline operations, reduce costs, and unlock new business efficiencies.

2024

Team Grows to 10+ Experts
