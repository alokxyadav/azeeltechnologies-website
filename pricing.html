Pricing FAQs

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before making a decision — India or international.

How does billing work?
Milestone-based billing via Razorpay (India) or Wise/PayPal (international). For projects: 30% upfront before work begins, remainder on delivery. For retainers: invoiced on the 1st of each month. GST invoice raised for India clients. USD invoices available for international clients.
Do you accept international clients and USD?
Yes — we actively work with clients from the US, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and Singapore. Payments accepted via Wise (bank transfer), PayPal, or direct wire transfer. Switch the currency toggle to see USD prices. Exchange rate used: ₹83 per $1 USD.
Are there any setup or onboarding fees?
No hidden onboarding fees. Prices shown are all-inclusive for the deliverables listed. The only additional costs are third-party tools like domain registration, hosting, or ad spend — always itemised separately and paid directly to the provider by you.
Can I cancel my monthly retainer?
Yes. After the minimum contract period (3 months for most plans), all retainers run month-to-month. Cancel with 30 days written notice. We deliver all work completed up to the cancellation date and hand over all files and access.
Do I own everything you create?
Yes — 100%. All code, designs, content, ad accounts, analytics data, and creatives belong to you after full payment. We never retain ownership of client work. We can also sign NDAs for sensitive projects.
How does the yearly discount work?
Paying annually saves approximately 20% versus monthly billing. Toggle the "Yearly" switch in the pricing hero to see annual rates. Applies to all monthly retainer plans. One-time project fees show a ~20% discount when selected in annual mode.
