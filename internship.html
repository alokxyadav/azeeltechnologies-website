Eligibility

Who Should Apply?

We value potential and passion over pedigree. If you're eager to learn and grow, you belong here.

 You're a Great Fit If...

  • ✓Currently enrolled in BCA, B.Tech, MCA, MBA, BBA, B.Sc, B.A. or equivalent
  • ✓In your 2nd, 3rd, or final year (or recent graduate within 1 year)
  • ✓Have basic knowledge or strong interest in your chosen domain
  • ✓Can commit 20–40 hours per week during internship duration
  • ✓Have a laptop and stable internet connection for remote work
  • ✓Self-motivated, punctual, and eager to learn from feedback
  • ✓Have any portfolio, project, or GitHub profile (even small ones)

 You May Not Be a Fit If...

  • ✗You're looking for a completely passive, non-involved role
  • ✗You cannot dedicate at least 20 hours per week consistently
  • ✗You have zero interest in the domain you're applying for
  • ✗You're unable to communicate in basic English or Hindi
  • ✗You're not open to feedback, iteration, or learning from mistakes
  • ✗You expect a senior full-time salary without prior experience
💡 No experience? No problem. We hire based on attitude, curiosity, and growth mindset. Fresh students are welcome.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Azeel Technologies internship program.

Is this a paid internship?
Yes, absolutely. This is a fully paid internship with a stipend of ₹6,000–₹8,000 per month. The exact amount depends on your role, performance, and experience. Top performers can earn performance bonuses that push the total above ₹10,000/month.
Can I intern remotely from any state in India?
Yes. Our internship program is fully remote-friendly. You can work from anywhere in India. A laptop and stable internet connection are the only requirements. We also offer a hybrid option for students near Gaya (Bihar) or Imphal (Manipur).
When does the 2026 internship start?
We accept applications on a rolling basis throughout 2026. Once selected, your start date will be mutually decided based on your availability. You can also choose your preferred start date in the application form.
I'm a first-year student. Can I still apply?
Absolutely! We don't discriminate based on year of study. If you have the skills, the passion, and the time commitment, you're welcome to apply. We have successfully trained first-year students who went on to do exceptional work.
Will I receive a certificate at the end?
Yes. Upon successful completion, all interns receive an official performance-based completion certificate from Azeel Technologies. Top performers may also receive a Letter of Recommendation (LOR).
What is a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO)?
Exceptional interns who consistently deliver quality work are offered a full-time position at Azeel Technologies upon the completion of their internship or graduation — without going through another interview process.
How many hours per day will I need to work?
We offer both part-time (20–25 hrs/week) and full-time (40 hrs/week) internship tracks. You can choose based on your college schedule. We believe in outcomes over hours.
Is the stipend transferred online?
Yes. Stipends are transferred digitally via bank transfer or UPI on a monthly basis (by the 7th of each month). All payment details are documented in the official internship agreement you sign before starting.
