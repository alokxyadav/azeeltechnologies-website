01 — Founder Story

Alok Yadav · Founder & CEO · Azeel Technologies, India

The Founder Behind
Azeel Technologies

"Technology built with purpose creates businesses that last."

From Curiosity to a Digital Solutions Company

Alok Yadav Founder & CEO of Azeel Technologies, a digital solutions company dedicated to helping startups and established businesses grow through modern web development, AI automation, and data-driven digital marketing. Originally from Gaya ji, Bihar, Alok is currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Senapati, Manipur, combining strong academic foundations with hands-on industry experience.

His journey began with a straightforward question: why do so many Indian businesses struggle to establish a credible, high-performing digital presence? That question drove him to master full-stack web development, SEO optimisation, and business automation — skills he quickly put to work for real clients across India, delivering measurable results rather than theoretical solutions.

Recognising the growing demand for reliable, affordable, and scalable technology partnerships — especially among Indian startups and MSMEs — Alok founded Azeel Technologies in 2025. The company was built on a clear mandate: deliver the kind of enterprise-grade digital infrastructure that helps businesses compete, grow, and thrive in an increasingly digital economy.

Today, Azeel Technologies serves clients across India and internationally, with a portfolio spanning custom web applications, AI-powered automation workflows, integrated payment solutions via Razorpay, performance SEO strategies, and full-funnel digital marketing across Google Ads and Meta platforms.
