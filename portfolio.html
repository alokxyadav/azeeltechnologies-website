Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Azeel Technologies.

What digital services does Azeel Technologies offer?
We provide web development, mobile apps, SEO, AI automation, WhatsApp bots, Meta Ads, Google Ads, digital marketing, video editing, and content creation — all under one roof in Bihar, India.
How long does SEO take to show results?
For most clients we see measurable ranking improvements within 60–90 days. Significant traffic growth typically occurs within 3–5 months depending on competition and domain authority.
What industries does Azeel Technologies serve?
We serve healthcare, finance, education, e-commerce, manufacturing, real estate, logistics, retail, media, and hospitality — 8+ industries with tailored solutions for each sector.
How much does a website cost at Azeel Technologies?
Pricing varies by project scope. We offer transparent milestone-based billing with 30–50% upfront deposit. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote tailored to your requirements.
Do you work with startups and small businesses?
Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes — from early-stage startups to established enterprises. Our solutions scale with your business and budget with no hidden fees.
Where is Azeel Technologies located?
Our headquarters is in Dobhi, Gaya Ji, Bihar, India (824220) and our development centre is at IIIT Manipur, Imphal. We serve clients across India and internationally via remote collaboration.
