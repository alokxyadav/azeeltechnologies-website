FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from our clients and prospects.

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on working days (Monday to Saturday). For urgent matters, we recommend reaching out via WhatsApp at +91 6009 590 154 for a faster response.

What does the free strategy session include?

Our free 30-minute strategy session is a no-obligation consultation where we understand your business goals, discuss your digital challenges, and recommend the most effective solutions. You'll receive actionable insights and a clear project roadmap — completely free of charge.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

Absolutely. While we are based in India, we work with clients worldwide. Our team is experienced in collaborating across time zones and delivering projects for clients in the US, UK, UAE, and beyond. All communication is conducted in English.

What information should I have ready before contacting you?

Don't worry if you don't have everything figured out — we're happy to help at any stage. If possible, it helps to share: (1) what you're trying to achieve, (2) your approximate budget range, (3) your preferred timeline, and (4) any inspiration or reference examples. But even just a project idea is enough to get started.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies by project scope, complexity, and service type. We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects and monthly retainers. Visit our Pricing page for indicative packages, or contact us for a custom quote tailored to your exact requirements.

Do you offer ongoing support after project delivery?

Yes! We believe in long-term partnerships. After project delivery, we offer maintenance retainers, performance monitoring, content updates, and continuous improvement services. We stay with you and your business well beyond launch day.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Of course! You can view our work on the Portfolio page. We've delivered 30+ projects across industries including healthcare, education, e-commerce, fintech, and more. Client case studies are available upon request.

Is information I share with you kept confidential?

Yes, absolutely. All information you share with Azeel Technologies is treated with strict confidentiality. We are happy to sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) before any detailed project discussions if required. Your business information will never be shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
